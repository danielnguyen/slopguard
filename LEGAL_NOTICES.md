## AI and heuristic limitations

SlopGuard may use heuristic rules, cached results, visible page metadata, and optional model-assisted classification. These methods have known limitations:

- page metadata may be incomplete or contaminated by YouTube UI changes
- titles and snippets may omit important context
- model outputs may be wrong or inconsistent
- cached results may become stale
- labels may over- or under-classify content
- sponsored-placement detection may fail when platform markup changes

## Recommended public-language standard

For public builds, documentation, screenshots, and store listings, prefer neutral pattern language such as:

- Check sourcing
- Context needed
- High-engagement framing
- Low-transparency signals
- Sponsored placement
- Commentary / reaction

Avoid presenting labels as definitive claims such as:

- fake news
- misinformation
- propaganda
- content farm
- scam
- foreign influence
- AI-generated
- deceptive creator

unless those claims are independently verified and appropriately sourced outside the extension.
